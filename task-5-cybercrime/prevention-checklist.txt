# Stay Safe Online – Checklist
*Prevention Tips
-Use strong passwords
-create unique passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols for every account.
-Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
-Always turn on 2FA for email, banking, and social media accounts.
-Avoid clicking unknown links
-Do not open suspicious links from emails, messages, or social media.
-Verify before downloading apps/files
-Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.
-Keep your devices updated
-Regularly update your phone and laptop to fix security vulnerabilities.
-Do not share personal information publicly
-Avoid posting your address, phone number, or college ID details online.
*UPI Safety Tip
-Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
-Always check the receiver’s name before sending money and avoid “collect request” scams.
 *If Something Goes Wrong (Report Immediately)
-Report online at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
-Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
