# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist 
* Do’s
Use strong and secure passwords for all your accounts
Think before posting (check if content is appropriate and respectful)
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news
Maintain privacy settings and limit personal information
Use social media for learning, networking, and positive engagement

* Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive personal details (address, phone number, etc.)
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative comments
Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages
Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
Don’t spend excessive time on social media affecting studies
